Here's to staying on task...

Some of us get distracted pretty easily.
After you click the "Get Started" button, you have 15 minutes to be productive.
At the end, you get an alert.
You're asked if you were productive during the past 15 minutes.

If you have, you get a picture of a happy cat.
If not, you get a disappointed cat haunting your screen.

AVOID THE SAD CAT AT ALL COSTS!

When your 15 minutes is up, simply reset by pressing the "Get Started" button and repeat!
